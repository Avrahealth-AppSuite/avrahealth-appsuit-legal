Privacy Policy

ClinicLoad (Workload-Insight)
Effective Date: March 10, 2026
Last Updated: March 10, 2026
Developer: AvraHealth

Introduction

AvraHealth (“we,” “us,” or “our”) built the ClinicLoad app (“Workload-Insight”) as a productivity tool for physicians to quantify, track, and manage their clinical workload. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information You Provide

All data in ClinicLoad is entered voluntarily by you. This may include:

  • Work Schedule Data: Clinic days and hours, OR schedule details, messaging volumes, administrative time, call and coverage details, academic commitments, and unexpected work hours.
  • Professional Information: Medical specialty.
  • Compensation Information: Annual salary and contracted weekly hours (used solely for on-device calculations such as effective hourly rate and FTE equivalents).
  • Contract Details: Contracted clinic days, OR days, patients per week, admin hours, and call frequency.
  • Goals and Preferences: Target workload percentage, target weekly hours, and notification preferences.
  • Weekly Snapshots: Historical workload data you choose to save, including demand hours, capacity hours, and category breakdowns.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other personal identifiers.
  • We do not collect patient data or protected health information (PHI).
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage analytics.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, photos, contacts, location, or health data.

How Your Data Is Stored

  • On-Device Storage: All data is stored locally on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework. We do not operate external servers, and your data is never transmitted to us.
  • iCloud Sync (Optional): If you have iCloud enabled on your device, your data may sync across your Apple devices via Apple’s CloudKit service. This sync is governed by Apple’s iCloud Terms and Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively for the following on-device purposes:

  • Calculating your weekly workload demand versus capacity.
  • Tracking workload trends over time.
  • Generating professional negotiation PDF reports.
  • Analyzing compensation and effective hourly rates.
  • Comparing your workload against specialty benchmarks.
  • Delivering local notifications for workload alerts (if enabled by you).

Data Sharing

  • We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your data to any third party.
  • PDF Export: You may choose to generate and share a PDF summary of your workload data using the standard iOS share sheet. Any sharing of that PDF is initiated and controlled entirely by you.
  • No Third-Party Services: The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing services.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data persists on your device for as long as you keep the app installed.
  • You can delete individual weekly snapshots within the app at any time.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
  • To remove data synced via iCloud, you can manage your iCloud storage through your device’s Settings.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications for high workload alerts and sudden workload increase warnings. You can enable or disable notifications at any time through the app’s settings or your device’s notification settings.

Children’s Privacy

ClinicLoad is designed for medical professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Security

Your data is protected by the security measures built into your Apple device, including device encryption and passcode/biometric protection. Since all data remains on your device (or in your personal iCloud account), there are no external servers to secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your information. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times.

California Residents (CCPA)

We do not sell personal information. Since all data is stored on your device and we do not collect or transmit it, CCPA data-access and deletion requests are fulfilled by your direct control over the app and device.

European Residents (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, the legal basis for processing your data is your explicit consent (by entering data into the app). Since we do not collect or transmit your data to any server, your data remains entirely under your control.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this policy.
